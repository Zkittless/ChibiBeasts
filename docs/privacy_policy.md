# ChibiBeasts — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 2026

---

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains what data ChibiBeasts collects, why we collect it, how it is stored, and your rights regarding that data. By using ChibiBeasts, you agree to the practices described here.

We take your privacy seriously. ChibiBeasts is designed to collect only what is necessary for the game to function.

---

## 2. What Data We Collect

ChibiBeasts collects and stores the following data when you interact with the bot:

**Automatically collected when you use the bot:**
- Your Discord User ID
- Your Discord username (at the time of first interaction, updated on use)
- Your Discord server (guild) ID, for guild-specific features

**Generated through gameplay:**
- Game progression data (level, experience, gold, Celestial Shards)
- Beast collection data (beasts owned, stats, nicknames, equipment)
- Inventory contents (items, eggs, materials)
- Battle and raid history
- Quest and achievement progress
- Guild membership and contribution records
- Trade history

**We do not collect:**
- Your email address
- Your real name
- Your IP address
- Payment information of any kind
- Any data from private Discord messages
- Any data from servers the bot is not active in

---

## 3. How We Use Your Data

Data collected by ChibiBeasts is used solely to:

- Provide and operate the game (tracking your progress, beasts, and inventory)
- Associate your game state with your Discord identity across sessions
- Enable multiplayer features such as trading, guilds, and leaderboards
- Detect and prevent abuse, cheating, or violations of our Terms of Service
- Improve game balance and features through aggregate, anonymized usage patterns

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.

---

## 4. Data Storage

Game data is stored in a secure database hosted on Railway, a cloud infrastructure provider. Data is retained for as long as your account is active or as long as necessary to provide the service.

ChibiBeasts does not use cookies. All data is server-side and associated with your Discord User ID.

---

## 5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties, except in the following limited circumstances:

- **Infrastructure providers:** Our hosting provider (Railway) processes data as part of running the service. They are bound by their own privacy policies and do not have access to your data for their own purposes.
- **Legal requirements:** We may disclose data if required to do so by law, regulation, or valid legal process.
- **Safety:** We may share data if we believe in good faith that disclosure is necessary to prevent harm to any person.

Certain in-game data is visible to other players by design — for example, your username may appear on leaderboards, in trade histories, or in guild rosters. Beast nicknames you assign are visible to others in shared contexts. Do not include personal information in beast nicknames or other user-generated content.

---

## 6. Data Retention and Deletion

Your game data is retained as long as your account is active. If you would like your data deleted, please contact us via our support server. We will process deletion requests within a reasonable timeframe.

Note that some data (such as trade history involving other users, or guild contribution records) may be retained in aggregate or anonymized form even after individual deletion, where necessary to maintain the integrity of other users' records.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

ChibiBeasts is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from users under 13. If we become aware that a user under 13 has provided data, we will delete it promptly. If you believe a child under 13 has used the bot, please contact us.

---

## 8. Your Rights

Depending on where you are located, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data, including:

- **Access:** The right to request a copy of the data we hold about you
- **Correction:** The right to request correction of inaccurate data
- **Deletion:** The right to request deletion of your data
- **Portability:** The right to receive your data in a portable format

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us via our support server. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe. Note that some rights may be limited by legal obligations or the technical nature of the service.

---

## 9. Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no system is perfectly secure. We cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data and are not liable for breaches outside our reasonable control.

---

## 10. Third-Party Services

ChibiBeasts operates within Discord. Your use of Discord is governed by Discord's own Terms of Service and Privacy Policy, which we encourage you to review. We are not responsible for Discord's data practices.

ChibiBeasts may use Cloudinary for image hosting (beast artwork). Images served from Cloudinary do not contain personal data.

---

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the updated policy and revise the "last updated" date at the top. Continued use of ChibiBeasts after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy. For significant changes, we will make reasonable efforts to notify users via our support server.

---

## 12. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, wish to exercise your data rights, or need to report a privacy concern, please contact us via our official support server.
